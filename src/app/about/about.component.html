About DSA Visualizer

DSA Visualizer is an interactive tool designed to help students and developers understand Data Structures and Algorithms through visual representations and animations.

Our goal is to make learning DSA concepts more engaging and accessible to everyone.

Features

  • Interactive visualizations of sorting algorithms
  • Step-by-step explanations of algorithm processes
  • Customizable input for algorithms
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile devices
